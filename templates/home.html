Video game preservation platform

Lutris helps you install and play video games from all eras and from most gaming systems. By leveraging and combining existing emulators, engine re-implementations and compatibility layers, it gives you a central interface to launch all your games.

The goal of Lutris is to keep our legacy of video games running on our own hardware. Video games are a significant part of our cultural heritage and are to be considered more than just an entertainment products. Video games have had an influence on every other art form, We have experienced video games across multiple generations of hardware and software. The fast changing pace of technology relegates games that are still of value to relics of the past that would only have worth in their nostalgic values. Lutris removes that barrier and aims to provide the best gaming experience for every title

Video games have existed on every digital device with a screen and some inputs. We are lucky to have software that is capable of recreating the environment games need to run on. We do not believe long term software conservancy is possible on a closed platform, which is why Lutris is stronly tied to the desktop Linux platform. Open source technology will allow you to build a gaming machine capable of standing the test of time and containing your very own legacy of games, just like you would have for your music or movie collection.
